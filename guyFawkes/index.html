Guy Fawkes - Game Idea

Story

The goal in the game is to help Guy Fawkes and Robert Catesby where player 1 is Guy Fawkes,
and Player 2 is Robert Catesby to kill the King of England, by exploding powder from a cellar beneath the parliament.
First you have to gather the team, by doing missions, get contacts, and get to know and trust eachother.
Once you meet, the second player can then join the game.
You will have gather alot of powder, or gunpowder to be exact.
You will either steal these barrels, or buy them.
They cost alot, if you choose to steal them, only one place in England have the right type of gunpowder.
You will have to guess which type of powder is the right one,
